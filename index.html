  • Nexxus Energy Group

    Company Background

    Nexxus Energy are one of the new generation of global companies within the oil and gas sector –operating worldwide and trading physical oil and gas products. With Macquarie Bank and StoneX asour financial partners, Nexxus Energy have the financial resource to execute trades of varying size orscale in multiple geographical locations.

    Nexxus Energy are also highly committed to the Carbon Disclosure Project (CDP) (2017) along with many of the leading oil and gas companies on the Climate A List. We welcome the opportunity to work alongside industry partners across various disciplines in a move towards the decarbonisation of the world energy system.

    Nexxus Energy was formerly known as Endeavour Energy Partners during the period of time the company was initially financially underpinned by private equity business - Endeavour Capital of New Zealand. When the opportunity arose for the majority shareholder (UFG) to undertake a stock repurchase of Endeavour Capital's minority shareholding, UFG moved to increase their shareholding to 100% outright ownership of the company.

    Nexxus Energy’s rise to becoming an active player within the market has been a formidable, yet rewarding adventure. We are the first to identify that this sector can be challenging and definitely not without its risks. Over and above delivering our financial targets, our shareholders are committed to the future and we have agreed a strategy to re-invest a portion of our profits into new low carbon start-up companies. This will be achieved through collaboration with Business Accelerators, Incubators and Academia. What’s encouraging is that this strategy has piqued the interest of some of our counterparts within this sector who are equally determined to build a sustainable future for the next generations; namely, a low carbon economy.

    -Gordon Cowan, Chief Executive Officer

  • KYC & Compliance:

    Nexxus Energy operate their business using a very simple approach: All trading counterparties will be asked to submit initial KYC/CIS documentation, which will be checked by our inhouse compliance team and later shared with our banking partners. Counterparties may be asked to show financial capability and suppliers may be asked to show proof of past performance. This will be on a transaction-by-transaction basis and will be dependent on our compliance teams’ findings once due diligence and credit profiling has been completed.

    Nexxus Energy can demonstrate their financial capability through our banking partners and in turn we may request our supplying counterparty to show verifiable evidence of their ability to supply or proof of past performance, again, on a case-by-case basis.

    Our counterparty onboarding & due diligence process covers the following criteria:

    • Know Your Customer – UBO & Corporate Structure
    • Sanctions Risk Exposure
    • Political Exposure
    • Performance Risk Analysis
    • Banking Due Diligence
    • Trade Reputation Profiling
    • Credit Risk
    • Bribery and Corruption Policy
    • Anti-Money Laundering
    • Privacy & Confidentiality
